John and Liked His Neighbors

John liked to walk up the road and visit his neighbors.

Pam loved John Henry and always had snacks ready for him.
Her house was always his first stop when he went visiting.

John eating snacks

"Bright Davis"

Adrienne would talk to John Henry through her kitchen window while she did her dishes. She was always glad to see him.

Adrienne


Bob Roberts lives at the very end of the road. It was too far for John Henry to go, so Bob brought John a bag of apples every week.

Allan owns the nearby Whitehorse feed store. He always stopped to visit and give John a flake of hay when he drove up the road to deliver feed.
